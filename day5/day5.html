It is much easier to apologize than it is to get permission.” I meandered around Memphis before college, starting the first high school based TEDx conference in TN, volunteering in community based schools, and nerding out to math proofs. So, I am a programmer by training because I cannot graduate without declaring a major and computer courses are what can hold my attention long enough to complete a sequence, but I am looking for opportunities to combine my love for hands on projects, team management, and improving diversity in STEM when I graduate from UNC in May 2019.
